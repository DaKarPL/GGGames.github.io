SMART HEX

Dive into the world of Smart Hex, a hyper-casual puzzle game that’s easy to pick up but hard to master!

Colorful hexagonal tiles are stacked across the board, and your goal is simple: clear them all.

You can only remove tiles of the currently required color. Sometimes you’ll need to dig deeper, uncover hidden tiles, and plan your moves carefully to succeed.

With simple one-touch controls and endlessly engaging levels, Smart Hex is perfect for quick sessions or long puzzle marathons.

BLOCK FIT JAM

Think in three dimensions and free every piece.

In this relaxing yet challenging 3D puzzle game, your goal is to carefully remove interlocked blocks trapped inside compact structures. Each block is connected to others, and only the right choice at the right moment will allow the construction to slowly fall apart.

There is no rush — just logic, spatial thinking, and satisfying problem solving.
