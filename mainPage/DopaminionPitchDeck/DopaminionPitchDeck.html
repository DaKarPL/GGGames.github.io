DOPAMINION - Pachinko Roguelike


DOPAMINION is a dark fantasy pachinko-roguelike where physics-driven drops determine survival.

Players descend into a cursed dungeon, triggering chaotic chain reactions, collecting upgrades, and building wildly different runs every time.

Simple input → emergent outcomes → constant dopamine feedback.

● Roguelike built around a reimagined pachinko mechanic
● Physics-based gameplay with strategic decision making
● Each drop creates unpredictable combat outcomes
● Runs evolve through random upgrades and synergies
● Fast sessions with high replayability

Genre: Roguelike / Physics-based
Camera: Pachinko board + combat presentation

Core Gameplay Loop

1. Player enters encounter
2. Drops or activates pachinko action
3. Chain reactions trigger attacks, effects, and modifiers
4. Earn upgrades and build synergy
5. Progress deeper — or die and restart

Every run becomes a unique build experiment.
